This document describes the Income Tax Calculator page: purpose, structure,
components, and how it fits into the AS Brokers site. The page estimates South
African PAYE using SARS 2026/27 tax tables (Budget 2026).

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. PURPOSE & USER JOURNEY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The /income-tax-calculator page has three jobs:

  • Educate: Hero explains PAYE based on official SARS 2026/27 tables; user
    enters gross monthly salary and age to see tax and take-home.
  • Context: Editorial blocks cover retirement income tax position, what the
    calculator shows, why tax is not static in retirement, who the tool is for.
  • Convert: CTA "Want Help Improving Your After-Tax Retirement Income?" with
    "Complete the form below" → /contact. Quick links to WhatsApp and solutions.

Linked from the Calculators hub under "Tax & Estate Exposure" (Phase 2:
Secondary Risk Architecture). Supports retirement and estate planning
conversations.

5. CALCULATOR & EDITORIAL
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  • Calculator: IncomeTaxCalculator in py-12 section, max-w-4xl. Inputs:
    typically gross monthly salary, age; outputs: annual tax, monthly PAYE,
    effective rate, net pay (per SARS 2026/27 logic).
  • Understanding Retirement Tax: Purpose is to show what you keep after
    tax; in retirement, multiple sources (living annuities, dividends,
    interest, rental) taxed differently; overlap can push into higher brackets.
  • What This Calculator Shows: List (annual tax, effective rate, net income,
    test income changes); note that plans often fail due to underestimated tax.
  • Tax Is Not Static: Income sources, drawdown, investments, age rebates
    change over time; structure that looks efficient today may need review.
  • The Real Question: Not "How much tax?" but "How do I structure income to
    keep more, legally and sustainably?" Calculator shows what is happening;
    planning improves why and how.
  • Who This Tool Is For: Multiple income sources or retirement income mix.
