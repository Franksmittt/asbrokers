================================================================================
  PART TWO — THE PROJECT (PURPOSE & SCOPE)
================================================================================

Project name:      as-brokers (AS Brokers website and platform)
Repository:        https://github.com/Franksmittt/asbrokers.git

Purpose:           A single, cohesive digital platform that serves as (1) the
                   public marketing and lead-generation website for AS Brokers
                   CC, (2) a suite of regulatory-aware financial calculators,
                   (3) an AI-powered Digital Wealth Assistant, (4) a CRM for
                   staff and owners, and (5) a secure Client Portal for clients.
                   The platform is designed to present retirement planning,
                   insurance, estate planning, and Everest Wealth products with
                   interactive tools and clear paths to contact and consultations.

What was built:    One codebase spanning:
                   • Public website (hero, solutions, calculators hub, team,
                     about, contact, insights, regulatory compliance).
                   • Interactive calculator suite (retirement, tax, estate,
                     inflation, Everest product tools) with email-for-PDF where
                     applicable.
                   • Digital Wealth Assistant (AI chat with tool-calling and
                     optional RAG).
                   • Financial Health Quiz (shareable results, routing to
                     calculators and contact).
                   • Contact form with HubSpot sync and optional Trigger.dev
                     PDF automation.
                   • CRM (dashboard, Kanban, leads, clients, tasks, executive
                     view) with role-based access.
                   • Client Portal (wealth dashboard, My Advisor, documents,
                     messages).
                   • Login and role-based entry (Admin, Staff, Client) for CRM
                     and Portal.

Design mandate:    Luxury, trust-led experience: custom design system (void/
                   shark backgrounds, rim-light glassmorphism, teal and gold
                   accents, trust hallmarks such as FSP 17273 and 25+ years).
                   Mobile-first, responsive; no off-the-shelf theme.

Data currency:     Income tax and Amethyst living-annuity tax use SARS 2026/27
                   brackets and rebates. Estate duty, donations (R100k/R200k),
                   and other calculator logic use current legislation; review
                   periodically.

  ------------

  PART FIVE — LEAD GENERATION & MARKETING AUTOMATION
================================================================================

  Contact form
  ------------
  • Location: /contact (and embedded in lead modal where used).
  • Fields: name, phone, email, topic dropdown, consent, honeypot (anti-spam).
  • Validation: Zod schemas; server-side validation in Server Action.
  • On submit: sync to HubSpot (search by email, create or patch contact,
    cumulative lead score by topic). Custom properties (e.g. platform_lead_score,
    financial_inquiry_topic) must exist in HubSpot for full sync.
  • Optional: trigger Trigger.dev task to generate PDF report and email via
    Resend (Puppeteer renders /internal/pdf-report, Resend sends attachment).

  Financial Health Quiz
  ---------------------
  • Route: /quiz.
  • Steps: primary concern, age bracket, available capital (with shareable
    URL via nuqs: ?concern=, ?age=, ?capital=).
  • Routing logic: e.g. yields + capital > R100k → Everest 12.8%; retirement
    55+ → Amethyst; estate taxes → Annual Estate Reduction; links to
    calculators and Contact.
  • CTA can open LeadFormContext (modal) for capture.
  • State: Zustand (useQuizStore); nuqs for shareable result URLs.

================================================================================
  PART SEVEN — CRM (STAFF & OWNERS) — DETAIL
================================================================================

  Entry:            /login — choose role (Admin, Staff, Client). Staff choose
                   their profile (e.g. Johnny Farinha). Cookies: mock-crm-role,
                   mock-crm-user, mock-crm-staff-id. Redirect: admin/staff → /crm;
                   client → /portal.

  Data:             lib/crm-data.ts (unified API). When Supabase is configured
                   (NEXT_PUBLIC_SUPABASE_URL, SUPABASE_SERVICE_ROLE_KEY), reads
                   from Supabase (leads, clients, staff, households, tasks,
                   correspondence, notes). Otherwise uses mock data from
                   lib/mock-crm.ts. Kanban status updates persist to Supabase
                   via updateLeadStatus.

  Roles:            Admin (owner): sees all leads, all clients, all tasks; can
                   reassign any lead. Staff: sees only leads/clients/tasks
                   assigned to them. Client: sees only Client Portal (no CRM).

  Dashboard (/crm)
  ----------------
  • Pipeline counts: New, Contacted, Qualified, Proposal, Won, Lost (role-
    filtered).
  • Recent leads table: name (link to detail), intent, status, assigned, Fit/Eng
    score; links to Kanban and full Leads list.
  • Trust hallmark (FSP 17273) in header.

  Kanban (/crm/kanban)
  -------------------
  • Columns: New → Contacted → Qualified → Proposal → Won → Lost.
  • Drag-and-drop cards (LeadsKanban); each card: name, intent, service
    category, Fit/Eng, (for owner) assigned advisor. Click name → lead detail.
  • Status updates: updateLeadStatus(leadId, status); persists to Supabase when
    configured.
  • Service filter available (Retirement & Everest, Short-Term Personal/Business,
    Life Personal/Business, Medical & Wellness, Estate & Business, Claims).

  Leads list (/crm/leads)
  ----------------------
  • Table: Name (link to detail), Contact, Service, Intent, Status, Assigned,
    Fit/Eng. Role-filtered. Optional service filter. Empty state when no leads.

  Lead detail (/crm/leads/[id])
  -----------------------------
  • Full contact (name, email, phone), intent, status, service, Fit/Eng,
    assigned advisor, household (if any).
  • Reassign (owner only): dropdown to assign another staff member.
  • Correspondence: chronological thread (portal, email, WhatsApp) for this
    lead.
  • Notes: internal notes with author and date.
  • Tasks: tasks linked to this lead (or to client once converted).
  • Tabs/sections for clear layout (CrmDetailTabs where used).

  Clients list (/crm/clients)
  --------------------------
  • Converted clients; columns: Name (link to detail), Contact, Services,
    Advisor, Converted date. Role-filtered. Empty state when none.

  Client detail (/crm/clients/[id])
  ---------------------------------
  • Contact, services, assigned advisor, WhatsApp link to advisor.
  • Same correspondence, notes, and tasks as on lead side (continuous history).

  Tasks (/crm/tasks)
  -----------------
  • Two blocks: To do and Completed. Each task: title, due date, assignee, link
    to related lead or client. Role-filtered by assignee.

  Executive (/crm/executive) — owner only
  ---------------------------------------
  • Redirect if not admin. Pipeline health: total leads, clients, conversion %,
    in-pipeline count.
  • Recharts: pipeline and service mix (ExecutiveCharts).
  • Staff performance table: advisor name, leads count, clients count.
  • High-priority leads (e.g. Fit or Engagement ≥ 80) with links to lead detail.
  • Trust hallmark “FSP 17273 · Executive” in header.

  Service categories (data model)
  -------------------------------
  Retirement & Everest, Short-Term Personal, Short-Term Business, Life
  (Personal), Life (Business), Medical & Wellness, Estate & Business, Claims.
  Used for filtering and display; ready for intent-based routing when backend
  is extended.
