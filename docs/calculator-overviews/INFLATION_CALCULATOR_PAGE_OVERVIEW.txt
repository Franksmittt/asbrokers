This document describes the Future Value (inflation impact) calculator page:
purpose, structure, components, and how it fits into the AS Brokers site. The
page shows what today's money will be worth in the future after inflation.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. PURPOSE & USER JOURNEY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The /cost-of-inflation-over-time page has three jobs:

  • Educate: Hero and teaser set up "future cost of living", "purchasing power
    lost", "real growth required". Calculator translates a current rand amount
    or income into future buying power.
  • Agitate: Editorial blocks ("Understanding the Impact", "The Common
    Misconception", "The Critical Question") stress that nominal growth can
    mask eroded purchasing power; "Are Your Investments Beating Inflation?"
    block leads to Everest/contact.
  • Convert: Multiple CTAs to /contact ("See what consistent growth looks
    like") and WhatsApp; "Purpose and Next Steps" closes with structure, tax
    efficiency, income planning.

Linked from the Calculators hub as "Inflation Impact Calculator" (Phase 1).
Supports the HNWI narrative that real returns and structure matter more than
headline returns.

6. EDITORIAL SECTIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  • Understanding the Impact: Inflation as hidden risk; compounds over 10–20
    years; real terms vs nominal.
  • The Common Misconception: Investments can grow while buying power
    decreases; groceries, medical, fuel rise regardless of account balance.
  • What This Calculator Helps You Do: Bullet list (inflation on income needs,
    compare growth vs inflation, sensitivity to inflation, replace
    assumptions with projections). Blue "→" bullets.
  • The Critical Question: "Will your future income still support your
    lifestyle in today's terms?"; lower future value = nominal growth with
    less buying power.
  • The Real Question / Everest CTA: "Are Your Investments Beating Inflation,
    or Just Keeping You Busy?"; volatility vs consistent double-digit
    structure; link to /contact "See what consistent growth looks like" +
    "View indicative Everest Wealth product projections". bg-black/20.
  • Purpose and Next Steps: Calculator is for awareness, not prediction;
    closing the gap requires structure, tax efficiency, income planning.
