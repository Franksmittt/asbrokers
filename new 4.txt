Understanding Everest Wealth

What are unlisted share investments?
▼
Unlisted shares are investments in companies or holding structures not traded on public exchanges like the JSE. They are not repriced daily, not driven by market sentiment, and operate on fixed contractual terms — usually five years. Returns are paid as dividends rather than interest, which is central to both stability and tax efficiency.

Why are these investments not affected by market volatility?
▼
Because Everest products are not listed on any exchange, they are not repriced when markets react to headlines, interest rate changes, or geopolitical events. Your returns are fixed for the contract term. This means stable, predictable income without the emotional rollercoaster of public markets.

How does the tax work?
▼
For voluntary investments (12.8%, 14.2%, and 14.5%), returns are paid as dividends and taxed at a flat 20% withholding tax — not at your marginal income tax rate. For the Amethyst Living Annuity (compulsory money), growth inside the annuity is tax-free, and tax applies only to income drawn at your marginal rate. Both structures offer significant tax advantages over traditional interest-bearing investments.

Who is Everest Wealth?
▼
Everest Wealth Management (Pty) Ltd is a South African alternative investment and wealth management business (FSP 795 — Category I, II & IIA), established in 2002. They focus on private (unlisted) investment opportunities structured for individuals, trusts, and companies. Certain portfolios reference assets under management of approximately R1.5 billion.

What is FSCA 1.8 shares authority?
▼
I am authorised under FAIS to advise and provide intermediary services on shares, including unlisted share-based investments, under the relevant FAIS product sub-category (the FSCA "1.8 shares" authority). This allows me to structure and advise on these investments properly and compliantly, with full consideration of income needs, tax treatment, and estate planning.

Income drawn is taxed according to your personal tax position
Capital not drawn continues to earn the fixed return inside the annuity
Actual tax outcomes depend on your other income sources and structure
