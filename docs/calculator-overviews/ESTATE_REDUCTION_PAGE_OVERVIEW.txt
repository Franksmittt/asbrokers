--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. PURPOSE & USER JOURNEY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The /annual-estate-reduction-strategy page has three jobs:

  • Educate: Hero and teaser set up annual donations exemption (R100k per
    person / R200k per couple per year, no donations tax); calculator shows
    cumulative duty saved and estate exposure reduction.
  • Context: Editorial explains how the strategy fits (moving value out of
    estate, reducing executor fees and cash-flow pressure); how it works in
    practice (donations to family trust); what the calculator demonstrates;
    important context (not a loophole, long-term strategy, trust + will +
    R3.5m abatement). "Don't Wait" block: exemption doesn't roll over; earlier
    start = more moved out.
  • Convert: "Ready to Take the Next Step?" – help with trust, donations
    structure, will integration; "Complete the form below" → /contact. Quick
    links to WhatsApp and solutions.

Linked from the Calculators hub as "Estate Duty Reduction Strategy
Calculator" and from /estate-duty-calculator as the natural next step.

5. CALCULATOR & EDITORIAL
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  • Calculator: EstateReductionCalculator. Inputs typically current age,
    individual vs couple; models R100k/R200k per year moving out of estate;
    shows cumulative reduction / duty saved over time (illustrative).
  • How This Fits In: R100k/person/year (R200k couple) no donations tax;
    consistent use = practical tool for reducing future estate duty.
  • How It Works in Practice: Donations often to family trust; capital
    leaves estate but stays protected and earmarked for family objectives;
    power is discipline year after year.
  • What This Demonstrates: Effect of small, consistent donations
    compounding into meaningful estate reduction; figures illustrative, not
    exact prediction; same framework used in client discussions.
  • Important Context: Not a loophole or once-off; long-term strategy, best
    started early, reviewed regularly; combined with trust structuring and
    will (including R3.5m abatement) can materially reduce duty and executor
    fees.
  • Don't Wait: R100k doesn't roll over; couple from 50 donating R200k/year
    for 15 years moves R3m out; start at 60 = only R1m; "The earlier you
    start, the more your heirs keep."
