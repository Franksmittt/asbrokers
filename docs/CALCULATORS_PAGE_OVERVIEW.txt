This document describes the Calculators hub page: purpose, structure, components,
styling, content, and how it fits into the AS Brokers site. The page organises
all financial calculators and education into themed sections with jump links
and FAQ schema for SEO.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. PURPOSE & USER JOURNEY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The Calculators page is the central hub for every financial tool AS Brokers
offers. It has three jobs:

  • Present calculators in logical groups (retirement, tax/estate, life
    insurance, product quotations, education, business risk) so users can find
    the right tool without hunting.
  • Frame each tool as problem-led: "Key question" per section, tag + title +
    description + bullets + "Leads to" so visitors understand what the
    calculator does and what advice or product it leads to.
  • Drive contact and consultation: multiple CTAs (Request a review, WhatsApp,
    Get in touch, Assess your business risk) and quick links to solutions.

There are no dropdowns in the main nav; "Calculators" in the header links
directly to this page. The page is the single entry point for the calculator
suite.

11. FAQ SCHEMA (SEO)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  • Component:  FAQSchema (components/FAQSchema.tsx). Accepts faqs array of
                { question, answer }. Renders a <script type="application/ld+json">
                with FAQPage schema (mainEntity: Question/Answer). Escapes
                angle brackets and ampersands for JSON-LD safety.
  • On this page: calculatorFAQs with two items:
      1. "What calculators does AS Brokers offer?" – lists retirement, income
         in retirement, inflation, tax, estate, premium comparison, Everest.
      2. "Are calculator results financial advice?" – No; educational tools;
         for personalised advice, book a consultation.

  The schema is invisible to users but helps search engines show FAQ snippets.

12. STYLING & DESIGN SYSTEM
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

                 Business Risk). Void/shark consistency with rest of site.
  • Cards:       rim-light (glassmorphism), rounded-[2rem] (squircle). No

                 link text (cinematic-teal).
  • Typography:  Clear hierarchy: hero headline large and bold; section
                 headings xl/2xl; card titles xl or 2xl if featured. Body
                 gray-400/zinc-500, sm. "Key question" in zinc-500.
  • Spacing:     Consistent section py-16 (or py-8/py-10 for compact strips).
                 max-w-5xl or max-w-4xl for content. gap-6 in grids.
  • Motion:      CSS only: hover:bg-white/10, hover:gap-2, transition-colors
                 / transition-all. No Framer Motion on this page.
  • Links:       All <Link> use prefetch={false}. External WhatsApp uses
                 target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer".
