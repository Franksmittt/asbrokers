This document describes the Retirement Reality Calculator page: purpose,
structure, components, styling, and how it fits into the AS Brokers site. The
page educates on retirement capital shortfalls and hosts the Retirement
Savings Calculator (required capital for target income).

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. PURPOSE & USER JOURNEY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The /retirement page has three jobs:

  • Agitate: Headline "You're Not Saving Enough for Retirement" and editorial
    copy explain why traditional savings fall short and why income replacement
    (not lump sum) is the real goal.
  • Educate: Two idea cards ("Why it happens", "The real problem") and a
    lead-in block set up the calculator with present-value-of-growing-annuity
    logic; FAQ schema supports SEO.
  • Convert: The calculator (RetirementRealityCalculator) shows required
    capital; post-calculator CTAs drive "Calculate the growth rate required"
    (contact) and Amethyst Living Annuity calculator.

Linked from the Calculators hub under "Retirement Reality" and from Solutions
(Private Wealth & Yield). Part of the HNWI funnel toward Everest/Amethyst.
