This document describes the Solutions page: purpose, structure, components,
styling, layout, and how it fits into the AS Brokers site. The page is
structured to prioritise HNWIs and retirement/wealth (Private Wealth & Yield)
over insurance, medical, and estate (Phase 2: Wealth Protection & Fiduciary
Defense).

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. PURPOSE & USER JOURNEY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The Solutions page is the main hub where visitors who clicked "Solutions" in
the header land. It has two jobs:

  • Drive high-net-worth users toward "Private Wealth & Yield" (Everest Wealth
    products, retirement calculators, fixed-return private equity) via a
    dominant first section and featured cards.
  • Present insurance, medical, and estate as "Phase 2: Wealth Protection &
    Fiduciary Defense" in a visually subordinated block so they don't compete
    with the wealth focus.

There are no dropdowns in the nav; "Solutions" links straight to this page.
Section nav uses premium labels (Private Wealth & Yield, Risk Architecture,
Health & Integration, Legacy Structuring).

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. URL & ROUTING
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  • Route:  /solutions
  • File:   app/(content)/solutions/page.tsx
  • Layout: Uses the shared (content) layout. No page-specific metadata export.

Sub-routes under /solutions:

  • /solutions/personal-insurance   – Short-term personal (home, car, etc.)
  • /solutions/business-insurance   – Commercial short-term
  • /solutions/life-insurance      – Personal life cover
  • /solutions/business-life       – Buy-and-sell, key person

These are linked from the Phase 2 cards; the main solutions page is a single
Server Component.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. PAGE STRUCTURE (TOP TO BOTTOM)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  1. Hero section ("Engineered Wealth & Risk Architecture.")
  2. Sticky section navigation (SolutionsSectionNav) – premium labels
  3. Section 1: Private Wealth & Yield (focus) – large section, featured cards
  4. Phase 2: Wealth Protection & Fiduciary Defense – wrapper heading + three
     sub-sections (Risk Architecture, Health & Integration, Legacy Structuring)
  5. Bottom CTA block ("Stop Guessing. Start Engineering.")
  6. Footer (shared)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  • Background:  Dark void (bg-[#0a0a0c]). Gradient (from-blue-950/15) and
                blurred blue orb (top-right) unchanged.

      - Headline: "Engineered Wealth" on one line; "& Risk Architecture." on

                  first: "Bypass traditional markets and secure your capital

                  solutions. Full-service insurance and risk management when
                  you need it."

                  (compliance non-negotiable).
      - Hero image: ImagePlaceholder, 16/9, /images/solutions-hero.jpg.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
5. STICKY SECTION NAV (SolutionsSectionNav)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  • Component:  components/SolutionsSectionNav.tsx (client).
  • Behaviour:  Sticky under main nav (top-[72px]); IntersectionObserver
                highlights active section.
  • Labels (premium):
      - #retirement  → "Private Wealth & Yield"
      - #insurance   → "Risk Architecture"
      - #medical     → "Health & Integration"
      - #estate      → "Legacy Structuring"
  • Layout:  Horizontal scroll on small screens; flex-wrap on larger. Pills:
             rounded-full; active = white bg + black text.
  • Styling:  bg-[#0a0a0c]/95, backdrop-blur-xl, border-b border-white/5.

7. PHASE 2: WEALTH PROTECTION & FIDUCIARY DEFENSE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  • Wrapper:    Single heading "Phase 2: Wealth Protection & Fiduciary
                Defense" (text-xl md:text-2xl, uppercase, zinc-500) above the
                three sub-sections.
  • Sub-sections:  Three <section>s with ids #insurance, #medical, #estate so
                    sticky nav scroll-to still works. Slightly smaller headers
                    (text-xl md:text-2xl) and icon boxes (w-12 h-12).
  • Cards:      ServiceCard with muted={true}: bg-[#101014], hover:bg-[#121218]
                so they don't compete with the blue focus cards. Same
                rounded-2xl, accent borders (rose, teal, amber).
  • Grid:       Same sm:grid-cols-2 lg:grid-cols-3; no featured cards here.

  Risk Architecture (#insurance):  Short-Term Personal/Business, Life
  (Personal/Business), Premium Increase Calculator. Links to
  /solutions/personal-insurance, etc., /premium-increase-calculator.

  Health & Integration (#medical):  Medical Aid & Gap Cover, Wellness &
  Integration. Links to /contact.

  Legacy Structuring (#estate):  Estate Planning & Wills, Trust & Business
  Structure, Estate Duty Calculator, Annual Estate Reduction Strategy. Links
  to /contact, /estate-duty-calculator, /annual-estate-reduction-strategy.
