This document describes the Everest Wealth product hub page: purpose,
structure, components, and how it fits into the AS Brokers site. The page is
not a single calculator but the central selector for four Everest investment
options, each with its own calculator or detail page.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. PURPOSE & USER JOURNEY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The /everest-wealth page has four jobs:

  • Orient: Headline "Which Everest Product Fits Your Retirement?" – four
    options, one question: what does your money need to do? Trust points
    (Everest FSP 795, fixed returns, not market-linked, zero broker fees).
  • Guide: "Start here" – two questions: (1) Type of money (voluntary vs
    compulsory retirement) (2) What you need (monthly income now vs capital
    growth vs drawdown flexibility). Maps to 12.8% Strategic Income, 14.2%
    Onyx Income+, 14.5% Strategic Growth, Amethyst Living Annuity.
  • Present options: Voluntary capital income (12.8%, 14.2%) in 2-card grid;
    voluntary growth (14.5%) and compulsory retirement (Amethyst) in second
    grid. Each card: title, subtitle, description, features, CTA to
    dedicated calculator/page.
  • Convert: "Not sure which one fits?" – conversation for full picture;
    "Let's have that conversation" → /contact. Understanding Everest Wealth
    block (FSP 795, AS Brokers FSP 17273 intermediary, returns not
    guaranteed) + link to /everest-wealth/about. Quick links (about, WhatsApp,
    contact).

Linked from the Calculators hub as "Everest Wealth Product Quote Calculators"
(Phase 1, featured) and from home/solutions as the Everest product entry.
