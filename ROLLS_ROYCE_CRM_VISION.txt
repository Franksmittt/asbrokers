Imagine a CRM that's not just software—it's a seamless, luxurious extension of

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
GOAL
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Seamless experiences where:
  - Clients feel like VIPs in a private bank
  - Staff operate with AI-powered efficiency
  - Owners have god-like oversight

Build trust through ironclad security (POPIA/FAIS compliance, audit trails),
personalization (AI-driven insights), and transparency (real-time updates).
Integrate with partners: Everest Wealth (product data), Santam/Old Mutual
(claims APIs), SARS (tax calcs via secure proxies).

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
KEY PRINCIPLES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  - AI-Infused Luxury: Use OpenAI (gpt-4o-mini or similar) for agents that
    automate routines while maintaining human touch—e.g., prepping meeting
    briefs or suggesting personalized wellness plans.

  - Unified 360 View: Every interaction (WhatsApp, email, portal) in one
    chronological thread, with household mapping for families (e.g., linking
    spouse estates).

  - Mobile-First Elegance: Apps with Apple-inspired design (rim-light
    glassmorphism, Nightography orbs) for on-the-go access.

  - Compliance-First: Automated KYC/AML, activity logging, and red flags for
    regulatory risks.

  - Scalability: Start with the mock (cookies → Supabase Auth/Magic Links),
    evolve to handle 1,000+ clients.

Services to weave in: Retirement/Investments (Everest), Insurance/Risk
(short-term/life), Medical/Wellness, Estate/Business Structuring, Claims.
For each, consider what users want to see, do, interact with, and feel—
empowering clients with control, staff with efficiency, owners with confidence.

================================================================================
1. CLIENT PORTAL: THE PRIVATE WEALTH TERMINAL
================================================================================

Clients (high-net-worth retirees, business owners, families) want visibility
into their financial world without friction. They crave reassurance ("Is my
legacy protected?"), personalization ("Tailored to my life stage"), and ease
("One app for everything"). The portal should feel like a Rolls-Royce Phantom:
opulent, intuitive, whisper-quiet operations.

Enhanced features (building on existing mock)
  - Unified Dashboard: Real-time "Wealth Overview" with Recharts (portfolio
    growth curves, annuity drawdown projections). Pull live data from Everest
    APIs for holdings (e.g. 12.8% Strategic Income: monthly income, 10% bonus
    countdown). "Retirement Readiness Score" using calculator logic.

  - Service-specific modules:
    · Retirement & Investments: Interactive planners—adjust drawdowns
      (2.5–17.5% for Amethyst), simulate inflation. "What-If" scenarios; request
      quotes via AI chat.
    · Insurance & Risk: Policy hub—coverage details (home/car, key person).
      Upload claims docs; track status with timelines. Renew or simulate
      premium increases.
    · Medical & Wellness: Wellness tracker—gap cover claims, plans by income.
      "Your Health Score" with AI recommendations.
    · Estate & Business: Estate simulator—assets, duty estimates (R100k/R200k
      donation strategies). View wills/trusts; collaborate via shared notes.
    · Claims: Self-service submission—upload photos/docs, track disputes. AI
      flags potential issues.

  - Interactions & actions:
    · Unified messaging: Chronological thread (email/WhatsApp/portal) with
      AI-assisted replies (e.g. "Draft response to advisor's query"). One-tap
      WhatsApp/Call to "My Advisor" (bio, availability).
    · Document vault: Secure uploads/downloads (Supabase Storage, audit logs).
      AI OCR for FICA; auto-categorize (e.g. "This is a will—file under Estate").
    · Appointments & reminders: Book consultations; alerts (e.g. "Annual
      estate review due—simulate reductions?").
    · Educational hub: Personalized content from Sanity (e.g. "Semigration
      Retirement" tailored to profile).

  - Trust-building: Biometric login, E2E encryption, transparency logs
    ("Advisor viewed your file at 10:15 AM"). No call centres—personal service.

  - Tech: Supabase data/RLS; Recharts; AI for personalization (RAG-grounded).

Outcome: Clients see holistic wealth health, do self-simulations/uploads/
bookings, interact via AI/human channels, and feel like owners of their
legacy—boosting loyalty and referrals.

================================================================================
2. STAFF INTERFACE: THE PRECISION ENGINE
================================================================================

Staff (e.g. Johnny for estates, Petro for commercial insurance) want tools
that eliminate drudgery, automate compliance, and let them focus on advice.
They desire efficiency ("Quick handovers"), collaboration ("Team visibility"),
and insights ("Client red flags"). This CRM feels like the Phantom's V12:
powerful, responsive, effortless.

Advanced features (expanding mock CRM)
  - Pipeline & leads: Enhanced Kanban with AI prioritization (Fit/Engagement +
    capital). Drag-drop stages; auto-route by service (e.g. commercial claims
    to Petro).

  - Client 360: Detailed profiles with household mapping. Timeline of all
    interactions; AI-generated summaries (e.g. "Retirement capital: R5M; Risk:
    High inflation exposure").

  - Service-tailored workflows:
    · Retirement/Investments: Automated Everest quotes (Trigger.dev PDFs);
      track Amethyst drawdowns, loyalty bonuses.
    · Insurance/Risk: Policy onboarding; premium calculators; claims with
      underwriter negotiation templates.
    · Medical/Wellness: Gap cover simulations; wellness plan builders with
      AI suggestions.
    · Estate/Business: Trust setup; annual donation trackers; duty
      calculators with scenario sharing.
    · Claims: Dispute resolution dashboards; AI-flagged negotiation points.

  - Interactions & tools:
    · Task management: AI-assigned tasks (e.g. "Follow up on FICA upload");
      reminders linked to clients/leads; internal notes; one-click reassign.
    · Correspondence hub: Unified threads with sentiment analysis (e.g.
      "Client seems anxious—suggest reassurance script").
    · AI assistants: Pre-meeting briefs (portfolio, interactions, market);
      automated follow-ups (e.g. "Send premium increase comparison").
    · Mobile: Scan docs on-site; real-time notifications (e.g. "Client
      uploaded will").

  - Efficiency: Workflow automation (onboarding: KYC → Quote → Sign); referral
    tracking with rewards dashboards.

  - Compliance: Role-based access (staff see only assigned); auto-log audits
    for FAIS.

Outcome: Staff see clear pipelines/client histories, do automate/collaborate,
interact via AI/tools, and feel supercharged—reducing burnout, increasing
billable time.

================================================================================
3. OWNER DASHBOARD: THE COMMAND CENTER
================================================================================

Owners want total control ("See everything"), insights ("Business health"), and
scalability ("Grow without chaos"). This is the Phantom's cockpit: intuitive
gauges, predictive analytics, effortless command.

Executive features
  - Global visibility: All leads/clients/tasks (no filters); AI dashboards
    (e.g. "Pipeline Health: 80% conversion; Top Service: Everest at 40%").

  - Analytics & reporting: Real-time metrics (AUM growth, claim resolution
    time); predictive AI (e.g. "Forecast estate duty savings: R2M next year").

  - Staff management: Performance tracking (e.g. "Johnny's estate
    conversions: 95%"); reassign leads; role permissions.

  - Service insights: Breakdowns by category (e.g. "Medical Wellness: High
    churn—recommend AI retention campaigns").

  - Interactions: Executive alerts (e.g. "High-value lead: R10M capital");
    one-click audits.

  - Growth: Referral analytics; marketing (HubSpot) lead scoring.

Outcome: Owners see business at a glance, do intervene/forecast, interact with
AI insights, and feel in command—driving 25%+ efficiency gains.
