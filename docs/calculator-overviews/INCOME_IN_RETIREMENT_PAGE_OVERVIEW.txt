This document describes the Income in Retirement (life of capital / run-out)
calculator page: purpose, structure, components, and how it fits into the AS
Brokers site. The page answers "Will your money last in retirement?" via the
Life of Capital calculator.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. PURPOSE & USER JOURNEY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The /income-in-retirement page is a focused calculator experience:

  • Headline frames the question: "Will Your Money Last in Retirement?"
  • Subhead: most people overestimate how long capital will provide income;
    "Find out in 30 seconds."
  • LifeOfCapitalCalculator lets users enter portfolio value and monthly
    drawdown to see estimated capital lifespan (run-out).
  • Footer trust line (Albert & Johnny, FSP 17273) and links (Home,
    Retirement income, Contact) keep the page simple and conversion-oriented.

Linked from the Calculators hub as "Income in Retirement Calculator" (Phase 1:
Capital Lifespan & High-Yield Solutions). Complements the home-page RunOutCalculator
(which also models run-out and gates full actuarial PDF).
