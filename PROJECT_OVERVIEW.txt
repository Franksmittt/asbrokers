1. PROJECT OVERVIEW
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Project name:     as-brokers (AS Brokers website)
Repository:        https://github.com/Franksmittt/asbrokers.git
Framework:         Next.js 15 (App Router)
Language:          TypeScript
Styling:           Tailwind CSS
Key libraries:     Framer Motion, React Hook Form, Zod, clsx, tailwind-merge

Purpose:
  Marketing and lead-generation website for AS Brokers CC, an independent
  authorised financial services provider in South Africa. The site presents
  retirement planning, insurance, estate planning, and Everest Wealth
  fixed-return products, with interactive calculators and clear paths to
  contact and book consultations.

Design:
  Dark theme (#0a0a0c, #151518), glassmorphism nav on scroll, gradient orbs
  on hero, bento-style solution cards. Responsive (mobile, tablet, desktop)
  with frosted header (80% opacity) when scrolling.

/team

/solutions
  - Solutions hub grouped by category:
    Retirement & Investment Planning, Insurance & Risk Planning,
    Medical & Wellness, Estate & Business Structuring
  - Each item links to a dedicated page or calculator
  - CTAs: All calculators, Book a consultation

/solutions/personal-insurance
  - Personal short-term insurance: home, car, valuables, asset protection

/solutions/business-insurance
  - Business short-term insurance: commercial property, liability,
    business interruption, fleet

/solutions/life-insurance
  - Personal life insurance: death cover, disability, income protection,
    severe illness

/solutions/business-life
  - Business life insurance: buy-and-sell, key person, loan account,
    employee benefits

/everest-wealth
  - Everest Wealth product hub
  - Income options: 12.8% Strategic Income, 14.2% Onyx Income+
  - Growth option: 14.5% Strategic Growth
  - Amethyst Living Annuity (pension/RA money)
  - Trust points (Everest FSP 795, fixed returns, zero broker fees)
  - Link to "Understanding Everest Wealth" (/everest-wealth/about)

/everest-wealth/about
  - Detailed "Everest Wealth Explained" page
  - Why alternative investments in SA; regulatory architecture (Everest FSP 795,
    AS Brokers FSP 17273 & Category 1.8, Laudian); product suite; how returns
    are generated; risks; fees; tax benefits

/lab
  - "The Lab" – entry point for run-out calculator and links to other tools
  - Full Run-Out Calculator (portfolio, drawdown, lifespan; email for PDF)
  - More tools: Retirement Reality, Life cover needs, Estate liquidity

/retirement
  - Retirement Reality Calculator
  - Inputs: current age, retirement age, life expectancy, monthly income needed,
    inflation, growth rate, tax in retirement
  - Output: total capital required (present value of growing annuity)
  - Editorial content on not saving enough for retirement

/income-in-retirement
  - Life of Capital / Retirement Longevity Calculator
  - Inputs: capital, monthly income needed, return %, tax %, inflation %
  - Output: years capital lasts (or "sustainable indefinitely")
  - Year-by-year simulation with escalating withdrawals

/cost-of-inflation-over-time
  - Future Value / Cost of Inflation Calculator
  - Inputs: present value (R), inflation % p.a., time period (years)
  - Outputs: future cost, future buying power, purchasing power lost %

/income-tax-calculator
  - South African Income Tax (PAYE) Calculator (SARS 2025/26 tables)
  - Inputs: gross monthly salary, age (rebates for 65+, 75+)
  - Outputs: annual tax, monthly PAYE, effective rate, net monthly pay
  - Editorial on tax in retirement

/estate-duty-calculator
  - South African Estate Duty Calculator
  - Inputs: gross estate, liabilities, bequests to spouse
  - Outputs: executor fees, estate duty (20%/25% brackets), total estate costs

/annual-estate-reduction-strategy
  - Annual Estate Reduction / Trust Donation Calculator
  - Inputs: single (R100k/year) or couple (R200k/year), current age, planned age,
    growth rate
  - Outputs: donation period, total donations, estate duty saved (20%), trust value

/premium-increase-calculator
  - Insurance Premium Comparison Calculator
  - Two policies: enter monthly premiums per year from policy schedules
  - Outputs: year-on-year change %, annual totals, average annual increase,
    cumulative spend at 5/10/15/20/25 years

/everest-128-product
  - Everest 12.8% Strategic Income Calculator
  - Input: investment amount
  - Outputs: net monthly income (after 20% dividend tax), annual income,
    5-year loyalty bonus, total 5-year return, return on capital %,
    "Why dividends beat income tax" comparison

/immediate-higher-income-calculator
  - Everest 14.2% Immediate Higher Income Calculator
  - Input: investment amount
  - Outputs: gross annual return, dividends tax, net annual/monthly return

/everest-strategic-growth-145
  - Everest 14.5% Strategic Growth Calculator
  - Input: investment amount
  - Outputs: capital after 5 years (after tax), year-by-year gross growth,
    tax on growth (20%), net growth

/everest-amethyst-living-annuity
  - Amethyst Living Annuity Calculator
  - Inputs: retirement capital, drawdown rate (2.5%–10%)
  - Outputs: gross monthly income, estimated monthly tax (SARS 2025/26, 65+),
    net monthly income
