================================================================================
  AS BROKERS — HOME PAGE ONLY (SUMMARY)
================================================================================

Source: app/(content)/page.tsx

This file describes everything on the home page: structure, components, layout,
and styling. The global Nav and AppShell wrap the whole app (including the home
page) but are not listed here as home-specific.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. PAGE PURPOSE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The home page is the main landing: hero with tagline and an embedded AI chat
terminal (Digital Wealth Assistant), trust strip, dual pathway (protect vs grow),
full run-out calculator hub with email-for-PDF, solutions bento grid (with 3D
tilt and glare), stats, value props, CTA strip, and footer. It uses the Apple Pro × Samsung Galaxy design system (void/shark
backgrounds, rim-light glass, squircle cards, Nightography orbs, teal/gold
accents).

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. PAGE LAYOUT (ROOT WRAPPER)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Wrapper: <div className="relative bg-void min-h-screen pb-24 md:pb-0">
  - Background: void (#000000). Extra bottom padding on mobile.

- First block (above the fold):
  - <div className="h-screen min-h-[600px] relative overflow-hidden">
  - Contains: BackgroundOrbs (full-bleed) + Hero (absolute inset-0, z-10).
  - One viewport height; content scrolls inside Hero.

- Rest of page: sections stacked vertically. Each major block is a component
  or a <section> with heading + component. Anchors: #lab (calculator hub),
  #solutions / #wealth (solutions bento).

--- 3.1 BackgroundOrbs ---
Path: components/BackgroundOrbs.tsx

What it does:
  Ambient “Nightography” lighting behind the hero: large, heavily blurred
  absolute-positioned divs in Cinematic Teal (#008080) and Gold/Orange
  (#FF7F50) to create OLED-style contrast. Mouse-reactive (Framer Motion
  springs); subtle scale/opacity animation with Apple easing. Respects
  prefers-reduced-motion.

Layout:
  - Position: absolute, full viewport; no layout impact (pointer-events-none).
  - Three orbs: top-left (teal), top-right (gold-orange), bottom-left (teal,
    lighter).

Styling:
  - Blur: blur-[180px] / blur-[120px]. Large radii (e.g. w-[70%] h-[70%]).
  - Colours: bg-[#008080]/25, bg-[#FF7F50]/20, bg-[#008080]/10.
  - Motion: ease [0.25, 0.1, 0.25, 1]; duration 4–6s repeat.

--- 3.9 SolutionsBento ---
Path: components/SolutionsBento.tsx

What it does:
  Bento grid of solution cards: Retirement & Investment (large 2×2),
  Insurance & Risk, Business Structuring, Fiduciary & Estate, Medical &
  Wellness (wide 2-col). Links to /solutions#… and /everest-wealth. Icons:
  LineChart, HeartPulse, Briefcase, Scroll. Each card is wrapped in TiltCard
  for 3D tilt and cursor-following glare.

Layout:
  - Grid: grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-3 gap-4 md:gap-6.
  - Retirement: md:col-span-2 md:row-span-2. Medical: md:col-span-2.
  - Cards: rim-light, rounded-[2rem], padding p-6→p-12 depending on size.

Styling:
  - All cards: .rim-light, rounded-[2rem], hover:border-cinematic-teal/20
    or hover:bg-white/[0.07]. Staggered Framer Motion (initial opacity 0, y 20;
    whileInView; ease Apple, delay by index).
  - 3D tilt: TiltCard uses useMotionValue (mouse), useTransform (→ rotateX/
    rotateY), useSpring (stiffness 300, damping 30); max ±8°, transformPerspective
    800. Mouse move over card drives subtle X/Y rotation (Apple-style spring).
  - Glare: pointer-events-none absolute div inside each card; radial gradient
    (white/transparent) follows cursor for a physical glass sheen in a dark room;
    opacity transition with Apple easing.
  - Retirement card: internal teal orb blur on hover. Icon bg-white/10
    rounded-2xl; text-cinematic-teal. Links text-cinematic-teal.
  - Other cards: HeartPulse red-400, Briefcase gold-orange, Scroll teal-400.
  - Medical: gradient wash from-cinematic-teal/10.
